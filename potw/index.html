About

IJSO Guide Problem of the week is a weekly online challenge for curious minds and problem solvers. Every week, we publish a fresh set of carefully crafted problems and invite participants from around the world to submit their solutions. Whether you enjoy physics, chemistry or biology, this contest is designed to stretch your thinking and reward persistence. Work at your own pace, test your ideas, and see how others approach the same problems.
